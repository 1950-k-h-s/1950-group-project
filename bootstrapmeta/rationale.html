Rationale

Goal

The goal is to redesign the existing the lecture site for Comp-1950 web Developement and Design 2 to make it easier to navigate or scroll, more responsive to different devices based on our personal experiences.

Target Audience:

The students who are interested in Information Technology

Schedule:

The project Due Date is at 11:59 on July 7th,2019

Things that need improvement or Change(reasons to redesign )

  1. Font is really small on mobile
  2. The navigation bar and menu are really difficult to read or to press on mobile.
  3. The lectures’ page are too long. Headlines are at the beginning but when you are in the middle,difficult to go to top. Not easy to navigate through lecture
  4. Search button in header to find a headline
  5. Not Responsive to size
  6. Different icons for different link(for example a pdf link has an Adobe Acrobot icon beside it)
  7. Add more color set to the page (for example for Installation instructions to be in the box with different colors). Though it is an educational not a commercial but it is tiring to look at it for a long time
  8. Add Accordion or carousel (to make the long page shorter and easier to navigate)
  9. Page 404
  10. Add a separate page for installation guide to different introduced development tools, it can be part of a sitemap
  11. Also a sticky nav that sticks why scroll down for easy access
